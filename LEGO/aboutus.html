About Us

Once upon a time in 1945, a planet called Legonus crashed with planet earth, which caused Lego bricks and figures to be scattered over the earth and become a part of our reality. This created good and evil forces in the form of superheroes and villains, who are our little helpers. If you need supernatural powers to carry out your daily chores, we have superheroes ready to help you. And for those of you who have some work that you may not be so proud of, we also have some who do not ask moral questions before they perform the task for you. We are a company that was established in 1945, with a goal of hiring different superheroes and villains based on each person's needs. On our website you will find more information.
