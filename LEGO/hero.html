Your Daily Hero

Superman

Rating

★★★★

Supermans original world gets destroyed from a natural disaster. Before Clark Kent dies in the explosion as a baby his parents load him into a small alien spaceship that lands in Earth. He gets found by the Kent family and they name him Clark. Later with his years he adopts the name "Superman". Superman is probably the most righteous person that has ever existed, he strives to make everybody around him happy and lives for saving people and the world. He is probably the most powerful superhero of all everybody we have here, therefore he does not take any small missions but if the mission you have for him meets the requirements, you will know it is going to be done right. Superman has laser beam eyes, super strenth, super speed and the power to fly.

Batman

Rating

★★★★★

Batman is a wealthy person that is originally from Gotham City. A city full of criminals, where he himself saw his parents get murdered in front of him. He then worked out, taught himself a variety of fighting sports. With his enourmous wealth he built a suit and a big variety of weapons he uses to fight everyday criminals in Gotham City. The amazing qualities of Batman besides being a human being like you, is that he is a very serious and a dutiful human being. You can always count on his expertise and his amazing quality of planning. He is said to be the only person that could beat Superman in a battle! Batman is also one of our top tier superheroes so if you have a mission for him make sure it is something that he would want to do.

WonderWoman

Rating

★★★★★

Diana was earlier a member of a tribe of women called the Amazons, Diana is a loving women that strive to make the world a better place day for day. Don´t let her nice looks and pretty face fool you, she is as fierce as the other heroes in this website. She has a cowbow looking lasso that she can use to catch her enemies, although it is a lasso it also holds special powers since it is brought from Amazons. WonderWoman has ha tender heart for humans as she is deeply in love with Captain Steve Trevor. She can help you with everything you need in your daily basis that requires some superpowers. She is also a great listener, that will take into account every condition you have for the mission she is assigned.

Ironman

Rating

★★★

Ironman orginially from Malibu California is a genius that used to be the forefront of manufacturing military weapons for "Stark Industries". After seeing what his weapons was used for he pledged his life to protect everyday citizens from the dangers of the world and the universe. Ironma is a funny and social guy that will have you laughing on your knees while he is finishing up his mission. He may not fulfill all your conditions if you are the picky type, but he will surely finish the main mission with passion and will serve you the best customer service you have ever experienced. He can fly, shoot laser beams and he is also a human that makes him more relatable to you!
